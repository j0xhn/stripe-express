Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real?

Absolutely. We saw this blog post about fractional ownership and decided to do it differently. At first it may seem a little far fetched, but it makes a lot of sense and the more you learn about it the more you'll love it.

Are the packages above the only ones?

We offer packages for nearly any interested group size. This includes multiple payment options and in-between packages. Send your questions to info@xoisland.com.

When will the Island be ready to visit?

XO Island is already developed with full amenities, it just needs to be purchased. After we sell enough packages to purchase the island, we percieve it will take 3 months to get deed in hand and open the island up.

What Does Unlimited Access Imply?

It means that if your package hasn't been reserved two weeks in advance you are free to reserve it for as long as you wish. This comes with the annual membership fee of $90/person (which is discounted with group packages)

Have you been to the Island?

Not yet. We've spoken with the realtor and plan on going down in April. Want to come? John and Bryson speak Spanish fluently, and have visited every country in Central America and have a good feel for the culture.

Why the yearly fee?

The up-front cost is the one-time purchase price for the package. We need annual payments for regular maintenance and care of the island.
